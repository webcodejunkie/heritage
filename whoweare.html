Hi my name is Matt Swain, I was born and grew up in Sanbornton N.H. on a Dairy farm on Hunkins Pond Road.
My Grandfather, Frank Swain owned and operated a General Store and Butcher shop in Winnisquam near Mosquito bridge. He sold the store and moved to Sanborton to operate the Family farm full time in the late 1940's. My Dad, David, loved the farm and expanded and grew it and to this day is still miling 70 cows and running a herd of over a hundred! Myself along with two Sister, Robin and Betsy
