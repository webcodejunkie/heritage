We Are A Hospitality Included 0% Tip Establishment

In order to better serve our Heritage Farm Family, our servers recieve a non-tip depedent, predictable, hourly wage for their service,
The cost of which is reflected in our menu item prices. In doing so we are not permitted to recieve any tips or additional wages.
We apologize for any inconvience this may cause and thank you for your support and understanding in this matter.
